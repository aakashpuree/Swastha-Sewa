About Swastha Sewa

Your Trusted Health Companion

Our Mission

Swastha Sewa (स्वास्थ्य सेवा) was created to bridge the gap between complex medical, veterinary, and agricultural knowledge and the general public. We believe that accurate, accessible information about diseases affecting humans, animals, and plants is essential for a healthier world.

Why We Built This

In today's interconnected world, diseases don't respect boundaries — whether between species or continents. A farmer in Nepal needs to understand both crop diseases and livestock health. A veterinarian may encounter zoonotic diseases that affect humans. Students and the general public deserve reliable resources without jargon overload.

Swastha Sewa was designed with three core principles:

  • Accuracy — Every entry is based on established scientific understanding
  • Accessibility — Clean, beautiful interface that works on any device
  • Comprehensiveness — One platform covering human, animal, and plant health

What Makes Us Different

Unlike fragmented resources scattered across the internet, Swastha Sewa brings together 100+ carefully curated diseases in one unified, beautifully designed experience. Our cyberpunk-medical aesthetic is not just for show — it reflects our commitment to modern, premium information delivery.

Important Disclaimer

Swastha Sewa is an educational resource only. It is not a substitute for professional medical, veterinary, or agricultural advice. Always consult qualified professionals for diagnosis and treatment decisions.

Future Plans

We are continuously expanding our database, adding new diseases, improving search algorithms, and developing additional languages. Future features include offline access, personalized learning paths, and integration with local health services.
